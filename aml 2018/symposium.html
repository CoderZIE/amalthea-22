SYMPOSIUM

What is Symposium?

The Symposium at Amalthea provides a platform for experts, scholars, enthusiasts and prospective clients with common interests to share their expertise, research and advances. It strives to bring together prospective collaborators and allow them to have a constructive exchange of ideas and perspectives. The theme for Symposium this year is: ‘Advancing Solar Power Generation’.

Why ‘Advancing Solar Power Generation’?

Solar power is a very promising renewable energy source of power generation that can lead to a more sustainable solution of the energy requirements. In the next decade, India’s energy profile will have solar power as a crucial component. The Symposium aims to realize the immense potential and emerging opportunities in this sector by holding a fruitful discussion on the challenges,problems and possible solutions.

Subrahmanyam Pulipaka

Founder | Soreva Energy Group

Topic: Solar Energy Technologies: A Roadmap to Future Entrepreneurs

Subrahmanyam Pulipaka is the Founder of Soreva Energy Group and a founding member of Indo Rwanda Youth Leaders Forum. He is also the current chairman of Africa India Youth Energy Forum. He has been involved in active research on the reliability of photovoltaics since last 4 years and was one of the youngest researchers from India to present at international platforms like IEEE Photovoltaic Specialists Conference and Solar Energy Forum in Qingdao, China. He is the principal at Pilani Photovoltaics, an R&D organization aimed at nurturing and developing technological solutions to solve energy access issues in rural areas. He is also actively involved in green skill development activities and addressed various intergovernmental training programs. He is closely working as an investor and mentor with clean technology ventures based out of Rwanda and India.

Jaideep Malaviya

CEO | Malaviya Solar Energy Consultancy

Topic: Solar Heat for Industrial Process, the next sunrise market

Jaideep Malaviya is the CEO, Malaviya Solar Energy Consultancy and Secretary-General Solar Thermal Federation of India. He carries 22 years long experience in the solar energy industry as a Consultant, Researcher, technical writer and policy analyst associated with several Indian and International organizations. He was instrumental in initiating the National Solar Energy Helpline and ran successfully for 7 years for Ministry of New and Renewable Energy. Currently, he is executing a three-year project “Solar Payback” for the German Solar Industry Association as a part of International Climate Initiative (IKI) funded by the German government that aims to accelerate the deployment of Solar Heat for Industrial Process (SHIP). He has published over 25 papers.

Pranav Mehta

Chairman | National Solar Energy Federation of India (NSEFI)

Topic: Converting solar dreams into reality in India: A path ahead

A national scholar throughout his academic career, Pranav Mehta is India’s leading, nationally-recognized technocrat. He is the Chairman of National Solar Energy Federation of India (NSEFI), New Delhi, Co-Chairman of the Global Solar Council (GSC) and also Chairman of Solar Energy Association of Gujarat. Apart from his hands-on experience of implementing solar energy projects both big and small, he is advising renowned National and Multinational Companies and has been mentoring startups in the solar sector. A National Scholar throughout his academic career, he has industrial experience of 40+ years at senior corporate management levels in India’s top-notch corporations like Reliance, GSPC etc. mainly in the area of “Concept to Commissioning” of New Technology Projects and Sustainable Development.

Dr. Kasivisvanathan Chelvakumar

Visiting Professor | IIT Gandhinagar

Chelva is an educator, businessman, entrepreneur and human rights worker. Presently he is a Visiting Professor at the Indian Institute of Technology, Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India where he teaches mechanical engineering, mathematics and business. He supervises research in the areas of solar power in India and business valuation and post war reconciliation. He teaches mechanical engineering and entrepreneurship at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Jaffna, Ariviyal Nagar, Kilinochi, Sri Lanka. He also taught healthcare finance at University of California, Irvine, USA. His professional career in USA over the past four decades has been in the sectors of defense, energy, healthcare, software start-up, academia and civil war diplomacy. Chelva received a Ph.D. in Mechanical and Civil Engineering with a minor in Aeronautics from the California Institute of Technology and a Health Care Executive MBA from University of California, Irvine.

Sameer Patwardhan

CTO | PC Technologies, USA

Topic: Solar Energy Technologies - Transforming Academic Ideas into Commercially Viable Products and Services

Sameer Patwardhan is the Cofounder and Chief-Technology-Officer at PC Technologies USA, a company that manufactures proprietary solar cell kits and other educational products. He has over 10 years of research and development experience in new materials and methods for photovoltaics and solar fuels applications. He has published over 20 articles on computational design, synthesis, characterization and application of photoactive materials, and is best known for popularizing hands-on and inquiry-based experimentation in solar energy, internationally. He is an alumnus of IIT Bombay, TU Delft and Northwestern University.
